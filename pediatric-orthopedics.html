PEDIATRIC ORTHOPEDICS

Highest level of orthopedic care for your child’s bones, joints, muscles, and growing body.

Advanced pediatric technology with child-friendly infrastructure

24/7 pediatric emergency and critical care support

Complete care journey from diagnosis to recovery

Specialised orthopaedic care for children — because growing bones need growing expertise

Children are not just “small adults”. Their bones are softer, their joints are flexible, and their growth plates are still developing. This means injuries, deformities, or pain in childhood must be treated differently from adults.

At Starlings, our Pediatric Allergy Clinic offers complete evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment for bone, joint, and muscle-related conditions in newborns, toddlers, school children, and teenagers.

Our goal is to help your child stay active and pain-free and grow with strong posture and confident movement.

Conditions We Treat

✓ Bone & Growth-Related Conditions

  • Bow legs & knock knees
  • Flat feet or high-arched feet
  • In-toeing / out-toeing (abnormal foot direction)
  • Limb length discrepancies
  • Delayed bone growth
  • Growth plate injuries

These conditions are common in developing children and often improve with early specialist support.

✓ Fractures & Childhood Injuries

  • Hand, wrist & forearm fractures
  • Leg, ankle & foot fractures
  • Shoulder & elbow injuries
  • Sports injuries & playground accidents
  • Sprains, strains, bruises

Our orthopedic specialists use child-friendly fracture management to ensure proper healing with minimal discomfort.

✓ Hip & Joint Problems

  • Developmental Dysplasia of the Hip (DDH)
  • Hip pain, limping or instability
  • Knee pain during sports
  • Juvenile arthritis assessment
  • Recurrent joint swelling

Early diagnosis of hip conditions prevents future mobility issues.

✓ Spinal Concerns

  • Scoliosis (curved spine)
  • Kyphosis
  • Back pain in children
  • Bad posture due to screen time

We help correct posture habits and advise on strengthening routines.

✓ Gait (Walking) and Movement Problems

  • Delayed walking in toddlers
  • Limping without injury
  • Toe-walking
  • Uneven walking pattern
  • Pain after walking or playing

These issues often indicate underlying bone, muscle, or nerve concerns.

Pediatric Orthopedic Services

Our approach focuses on minimally invasive treatment, ensuring the fastest and safest recovery.

✓ Clinical and Physical Examination

Complete evaluation of movement, flexibility, joint stability and bone alignment.

✓ Gait Analysis

Assessment of how your child walks, stands, or runs to detect abnormalities early.

✓ Advanced Imaging Support

X-rays, ultrasounds or MRI (when needed) for accurate diagnosis.

✓ Fracture and Injury Treatment

  • Plaster casting
  • Splints & braces
  • Non-surgical realignment
  • Follow-up growth monitoring

✓ Limb and Foot Deformity Correction

  • Physiotherapy-based correction
  • Shoe inserts / orthotic support
  • Long-term growth monitoring

✓ Sports and Activity Injury Care

Customized rehabilitation plans to help children return safely to school, sports, and daily activities.

✓ Posture Correction Programs

Guidance to improve back posture, strengthen muscles and prevent long-term issues.

When Should You See a Pediatric Orthopedic Specialist?

Schedule a visit if your child:

  • Has persistent bone or joint pain
  • Complains of pain after running or playing
  • Walks unusually or limps
  • Has swelling or redness around joints
  • Shows leg deformities (bow legs/knock knees)
  • Frequently trips or falls
  • Has trouble standing straight or sitting upright
  • Has a suspected fracture after falling

Early evaluation reduces long-term complications and supports healthy growth.
