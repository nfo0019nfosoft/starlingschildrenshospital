VBAC Care That Prioritises Safety & Confidence

For many mothers, a previous cesarean does not always mean a repeat C-section. At Birthstories by Starlings, we offer VBAC services in Hyderabad for eligible women who wish to experience a normal vaginal birth after a prior cesarean delivery.

Our obstetric team carefully evaluates every case and provides expert guidance, ensuring the highest standards of safety for both mother and baby throughout labor and delivery.

What Is VBAC ?

VBAC refers to a vaginal birth after a previous cesarean section, performed under close medical supervision. With the right conditions, medical support, and continuous monitoring, VBAC can be a safe and positive option for many women.

Each VBAC case is individually assessed to determine suitability and minimize risks.

Who Is Eligible for VBAC?

VBAC may be suitable for women who:

  • Have had one previous lower-segment C-section
  • Do not have contraindications for vaginal delivery
  • Have no major pregnancy complications
  • Are under continuous medical supervision

Our obstetricians will guide you through the safest delivery option based on your individual condition.
