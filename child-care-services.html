Our Child Care Services

At Starlings Children's Hospital, we offer a comprehensive range of child care services covering preventive care, advanced diagnostics, and specialized pediatric treatments. Our services are designed to support children from birth through adolescence with expert medical attention and compassionate care.

Primary medical care for children of all age groups

General Pediatrics at Starlings Children’s Hospital provides first-level medical care for infants, children, and adolescents. This service focuses on preventive healthcare, timely diagnosis, and management of common childhood illnesses. Paediatricians assess growth milestones, monitor overall development, and treat conditions such as fever, infections, respiratory issues, digestive concerns, and nutritional problems. Parents are guided on vaccinations, routine health monitoring, and when specialist care may be required.
