Supporting Women Through Every Hormonal Transition

Menopause and perimenopause are natural phases in a woman’s life, but the symptoms can affect physical, emotional, and mental well-being. At At Birthstories by Starlings, our menopause and perimenopause care in Hyderabad ihelping women manage symptoms effectively and maintain a high quality of life.

Our women’s care specialists focus on understanding each woman’s unique experience and offering personalised, evidence-based solutions.

What Are Perimenopause and Menopause?

  • Perimenopause is the transitional phase leading up to menopause, marked by hormonal fluctuations and irregular periods.
  • Menopause is diagnosed after 12 consecutive months without menstruation and signals the end of the reproductive phase.

With the right medical guidance, these transitions can be managed comfortably and confidently.

Common Symptoms We Help Manage

Our specialists help women manage symptoms such as:

  • Irregular or heavy periods
  • Hot flashes & night sweats
  • Mood swings & anxiety
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Fatigue & low energy
  • Weight changes
  • Vaginal dryness & discomfort
  • Bone health concerns

Who Should Seek Menopause or Perimenopause Care?

This specialised care is recommended for women who:

  • Are experiencing irregular periods after age 40
  • Have persistent menopausal symptoms affecting daily life
  • Want preventive care for bone and hormonal health
  • Seek medical guidance for healthy aging

Early intervention can significantly improve comfort and long-term health.
