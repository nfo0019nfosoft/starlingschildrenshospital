Professional Lactation Care for New Mothers

Lactation is a critical part of postnatal recovery and newborn nourishment, but many mothers experience challenges during this phase. At Birthstories by Starlings, we provide specialised lactation care in Hyderabad, helping mothers manage milk production, comfort, and overall well-being during the postnatal period.

Our lactation specialists offer expert assessment, guidance, and reassurance in a calm and supportive environment.

Lactation Services at Birthstories

✓ Lactation Assessment & Counselling

Evaluation of milk production, breast health, and postnatal concerns.

✓ Milk Supply Management

Guidance for low supply, oversupply, and regulation of milk flow.

✓ Management of Lactation-Related Conditions

Care for engorgement, blocked ducts, mastitis, and breast discomfort.

✓ Postnatal Lactation Monitoring

Ongoing assessment during hospital stay and recovery period.

✓ Lactation Guidance for Special Cases

Support for mothers of preterm babies or those with medical conditions.

Who Can Benefit from Lactation Support?

Lactation care is recommended for mothers who:

  • Are experiencing breastfeeding difficulties
  • Have concerns about milk supply
  • Need help with baby latching techniques
  • Experience breast pain, engorgement, or discomfort
  • Have delivered preterm or low-birth-weight babies

Early lactation support can improve breastfeeding success and maternal confidence.
