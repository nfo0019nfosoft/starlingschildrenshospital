Compassionate Breast Feeding Support for New Mothers

Breast feeding is natural, but it can feel challenging in the early days after childbirth At Birthstories by Starlings, we provide specialised lactation care in Hyderabad, helping mothers overcome common feeding concerns and feel confident in nourishing their newborns.

Our trained support specialists focus on comfort, technique, and reassurance — ensuring a positive feeding experience for both mother and baby.

Breast Feeding Support Services at Birthstories

✓ Breast Feeding Assessment & Guidance

Evaluation of feeding technique, comfort, and baby’s response.

✓ Latching & Positioning Support

Hands-on guidance to improve latch and feeding comfort.

✓ Support for Feeding Challenges

Care for sore nipples, engorgement, and feeding difficulties.

✓ Support for First-Time Mothers

Step-by-step guidance and reassurance during early feeding days.

✓ Postnatal Feeding Support

Continued assistance during hospital stay and recovery period.

Who Can Benefit from Breast Feeding Support?

Breast feeding support is recommended for:

  • First-time mothers
  • Mothers experiencing pain or discomfort
  • Babies with latching difficulties
  • Mothers recovering after C-section
  • Mothers seeking feeding confidence

Support is always personalised to individual needs.
