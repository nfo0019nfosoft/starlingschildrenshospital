PEDIATRIC INTENSIVE CARE UNIT (PICU)

Advanced ICU care to treat critically ill children with 24×7 monitoring.

Advanced pediatric technology with child-friendly infrastructure

24/7 pediatric emergency and critical care support

Complete care journey from diagnosis to recovery

Expert critical care for children during medical emergencies

The Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU) at Starlings Children’s Hospital is built around one core principle — providing the right care at the right time for critically ill children. Every decision in our PICU is guided by clinical experience, real-time monitoring, and evidence-based pediatric protocols.

Our pediatric intensivists bring hands-on experience in managing complex medical emergencies, including respiratory failure, severe infections, neurological emergencies, cardiac conditions, and post-surgical critical care. Treatment plans are continuously reviewed and updated based on the child’s response, ensuring personalised and adaptive care rather than one-size-fits-all treatment.

Starlings follows standardised critical care guidelines, infection control protocols, and transparent communication practices, ensuring families understand every step of their child’s care journey. Parents are kept informed with clear updates, explained in simple language, because informed families build trust — and trust is essential in critical care.

Conditions We Treat in PICU

24/7 availability of pediatric specialists, advanced diagnostics, emergency response systems, and intensive monitoring, our PICU functions as a reliable support system during the most critical moments. This combination of medical expertise, advanced infrastructure, and ethical care delivery makes Starlings a trusted choice for pediatric critical care in Hyderabad.
