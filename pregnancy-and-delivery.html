Pregnancy Care That Puts Mothers First

Pregnancy is a life-changing journey that requires expert medical guidance, emotional reassurance, and continuous care. At Birthstories by Starlings, we offer complete pregnancy and delivery care in Hyderabad, ensuring the safety, comfort, and well-being of both mother and baby at every stage.

From your first positive test to the moment you hold your baby, our obstetricians walk with you through every milestone with personalised care plans and advanced medical support.

What Our Pregnancy & Delivery Care Includes

Our maternity team provides complete care throughout all stages of pregnancy:

  • Antenatal check-ups & pregnancy monitoring
  • Ultrasound scans & fetal growth assessment
  • Nutritional & lifestyle counselling
  • Management of pregnancy symptoms & discomforts
  • Birth planning & delivery counselling
  • Normal delivery & assisted delivery care
  • Caesarean section (C-section) when medically required
  • Post-delivery mother & newborn monitoring

When Should You Visit a Pregnancy Specialist?

You should consult a pregnancy specialist if you:

  • Are planning pregnancy
  • Have confirmed pregnancy
  • Experience pregnancy-related discomfort or complications
  • Have a history of miscarriage or C-section
  • Are diagnosed with a high-risk pregnancy
  • Need guidance on delivery options

Early and regular antenatal care ensures a healthier pregnancy and safer delivery.
