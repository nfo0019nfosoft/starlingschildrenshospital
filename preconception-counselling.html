Plan Pregnancy with Confidence and Clarity

Preconception counselling plays a crucial role in ensuring a healthy pregnancy and a safe childbirth experience. At Birthstories by Starlings, we offer expert preconception counselling in Hyderabad, helping couples understand their health, manage risks early, and prepare for pregnancy with confidence.

Our specialists focus on medical evaluation, lifestyle guidance, and emotional readiness — giving you the best possible start to your pregnancy journey.

What Is Preconception Counselling?

Preconception counselling is a medical consultation conducted before pregnancy, aimed at identifying and managing health conditions that could affect fertility, pregnancy, or the baby’s development.

Early planning helps improve conception success and pregnancy outcomes.

What Our Preconception Counselling Includes

Our comprehensive counselling program covers:

  • Complete medical history evaluation
  • Menstrual cycle & ovulation assessment
  • Fertility evaluation & guidance
  • Nutritional & lifestyle counselling
  • Management of existing medical conditions
  • Medication review & supplementation guidance
  • Genetic risk assessment (if required)
  • Emotional and mental well-being support

Who Should Consider Preconception Counselling?

Preconception counselling is recommended for:

  • Couples planning their first pregnancy
  • Women with irregular periods or PCOS
  • Couples facing difficulty conceiving
  • Women with previous pregnancy complications
  • Women above 30 years planning pregnancy
  • Couples with medical or genetic concerns
