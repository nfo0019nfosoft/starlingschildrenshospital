Dedicated Breast Care with Sensitivity & Expertise

Breast health is a vital part of a woman’s overall well-being and requires timely attention and expert care. At Birthstories by Starlings, our Breast Care Clinic in Hyderabad offers complete evaluation, diagnosis, and management of breast-related concerns in a safe, supportive, and confidential environment.

We understand that breast concerns can be both physically and emotionally stressful. Our specialists focus on early detection, accurate diagnosis, and personalised care — ensuring reassurance at every step.

What Is a Breast Care Clinic?

A breast care clinic is a specialised service dedicated to the evaluation and management of breast-related symptoms and conditions. It focuses on early identification of issues, preventive screening, and timely treatment to support long-term breast health.

Early consultation plays a crucial role in effective outcomes.

Breast Care Services at Birthstories

✓ Clinical Breast Examination

Detailed physical examination by experienced women’s care specialists.

✓ Breast Imaging & Diagnostic Support

Guidance for ultrasound, mammography, or further evaluation if required.

✓ Evaluation of Breast Lumps & Pain

Timely assessment to identify the cause and plan appropriate care.

✓ Pregnancy & Hormonal Breast Changes Care

Management of breast-related changes during pregnancy and hormonal phases.

✓ Preventive Breast Health Guidance

Education on self-examination and routine screening recommendations.

Breast Conditions We Evaluate & Manage

Our Breast Care Clinic provides expert care for:

  • Breast pain or tenderness
  • Breast lumps or swellings
  • Changes in breast size, shape, or skin
  • Nipple discharge or pain
  • Breast infections or inflammation
  • Benign breast conditions
  • Breast changes during pregnancy or lactation
  • Preventive breast health screening

Support is always personalised to individual needs.

When Should You Visit a Breast Care Clinic?

You should consult a breast specialist if you notice:

  • A new breast lump or thickening
  • Persistent breast pain
  • Skin changes such as redness or dimpling
  • Nipple discharge or retraction
  • Any unusual or sudden change in the breast

Early evaluation helps ensure peace of mind and timely care.
