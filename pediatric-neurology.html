Specialised brain, nerve & developmental care for your child

A child’s brain and nervous system are still developing, making them more sensitive to seizures, headaches, developmental delays, movement issues, and behavioural changes.

Starlings Pediatric Allergy Clinic provides comprehensive evaluation and treatment for neurological conditions in newborns, infants, children, and teenagers.

Our specialists use child-friendly assessments, advanced diagnostics, and personalised treatment plans to ensure accurate care and long-term neurological wellness.

When to See a Pediatric Neurologist

Visit Starlings if your child has:

  • Seizures or unusual jerking episodes
  • Headaches that keep coming back
  • Developmental or speech delays
  • Difficulty walking or repeated falls
  • Weakness in arms or legs
  • Poor attention or hyperactivity
  • Loss of skills previously learned
  • Tremors or involuntary movements
  • Concerns after a head injury

Early treatment improves outcomes and supports healthy brain development.
